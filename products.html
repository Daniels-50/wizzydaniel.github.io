WHAT WE PRODUCE

Mary Agro Farms has been a beacon of hope and prosperity for the community since its inception. Founded with the vision of providing nutritious food and sustainable farming practices, this humble farm has blossomed into a thriving enterprise, driven by the unwavering determination of its founder, Bishop Obodo. Since its establishment, Mary Agro Farms has transformed the lives of countless individuals within the community. Through the sale of its fresh produce, the farm has created employment opportunities for local youth and has also contributed to the local economy by providing affordable and nutritious food. But Mary Agro Farms' impact doesn't end there. The farm has also been at the forefront of promoting sustainable farming practices within the region. With a deep understanding of the challenges facing small-scale farmers, Mary Agro Farms has implemented innovative solutions that not only safeguard the environment but also improve the quality of life for farmers and their families. One of the most notable initiatives launched by the farm has been the "Farmers' Empowerment Program," which offers training and resources to local farmers, enabling them to enhance their skills, maximize their yields, and boost their livelihoods.
